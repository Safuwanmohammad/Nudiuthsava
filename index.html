About the Festival

Nudi Uthsava 2025–26 is a prestigious year-long district-level Kannada cultural and literary initiative organized by Nudi Uthsava Samithi, Kodagu District.

Scheduled from November 2025 to November 2026, this initiative is dedicated to promoting and preserving the Kannada language, literature, folk traditions, performing arts, and cultural heritage across Kodagu.

Vision

To strengthen Kannada language awareness, literary appreciation, and cultural pride among the people of Kodagu, particularly the younger generation, through sustained and inclusive engagement.

Mission

  • Promote Kannada literature, art, and folk traditions through district-wide events.
  • Create platforms for students, writers, and artists to celebrate Kannada identity.
  • Foster collaboration between educational institutions and cultural organizations.
  • Build a sustainable cultural movement preserving Kodagu’s linguistic heritage.

Programme Overview

Duration: March 2026 – November 2026

Total Events: 19

Scope: District-Level (Kodagu District)

Key Components

  • 3 Major Public Cultural & Literary Programmes
  • 1 National-Level Kannada Book Festival
  • District-Level Literary Competitions across 15 Degree & PG Colleges

Expected Reach

  • 7000+ Students
  • 1400+ Participants & Audiences
  • 12000+ Visitors (National-Level Book Festival)

Thematic Pillars

  1. Nudi Sahitya Lahari (Literary Waves)
    Seminars, poetry meets, book discussions, and a National-Level Book Festival celebrating Kannada literature and authors.
  2. Nudi Samskruti (Cultural Heritage)
    Folk performances, agrarian traditions, and indigenous cultural expressions highlighting Kodagu’s heritage.
  3. Nudi Geethe Gayana (Music of Language)
    Folk songs, Bhavageethe, Kavya Vaachana, and music competitions promoting Kannada lyrical traditions.
  4. Nudi Nrutya Sambhrama (Dance Festival)
    Inter-Degree College Kannada traditional and folk dance competitions showcasing regional dance forms.
